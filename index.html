v8.2 — 5-Layer Deduplication Engine
LAYER 1 — Tab Capture
getParaPlainText() converts <w:tab/> XML elements to spaces. AQA stores "1.[TAB]AND" — without this, regex sees "1.AND" and spacing checks fail.
LAYER 2 — Label Detection
isMCQOptionLine() catches: A) a) (a) 1) and all variants. Three AQA sub-cases: "1. AND" (2+ spaces), "1. AND" (1 space), "1.AND" (zero space after dot).
LAYER 3 — Dot Pattern
isOptionPara() regex /^[1-4]\.\s*\S/ — matches dot followed by zero-or-more spaces. Catches all AQA numbered list formats regardless of whitespace.
LAYER 4 — Justify Strip
Removes "Justify Your Answer" trailing lines that sometimes appear after MCQ options in AQA papers, preventing them from appearing in the DOCX output.
LAYER 5 — Content Match ★
Strips label prefix, normalises whitespace, then compares paragraph text directly against q.options[]. This is the most reliable layer — catches ANY format as long as option text was correctly parsed.
